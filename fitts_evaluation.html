Mouse Evaluation

Thank you again for participating in this mouse user evaluation.

This section of the evaluation is focused on seeing how easy it is to use your mouse. There are four clicking tasks in this section.

  1. Click circular target placed around in a circle with your cursor
  2. Click randomly placed circular targets with your cursor
  3. Select targets using the scroll wheel
  4. Select randomly placed targets using the scroll wheel

This section should take roughly 5 - 7 minutes.

Circular Left Click Evaluation

When you start you will see a ring of targets. Click the highlighted target as accuractly and as quickly as you can using your mouse and left click.

Random Left Click Evaluation

When you start you will see a single target. Click each target as accuractly and as quickly as you can using your mouse and left click.

Back and Forth Scrolling Evalaution

When you start you will see two targets. Scroll until the black bar is inside a target and click using the middle mouse button.

Random Scrolling Evaluation

When you start you will see a single target. Scroll until the black bar is inside a target and click using the middle mouse button.
